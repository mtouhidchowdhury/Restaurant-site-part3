Who are we?

Bengali Corner is an authentic south asian restaurant that serves fresh meals ranging from biryanis to fresh lentil soups. All the dishes cooked are ensured 100% freshness quality and give you a feeling of being back Home in the city of Dhaka for those who originate from Bangladesh. The dishes are also suitable for those who come from different backgrounds as we have both sweet and savory options. We hope to see you soon!
